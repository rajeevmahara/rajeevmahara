## 🚀 About Me

Most people learn to code inside a classroom. I learned mine inside `console.log()` and broken builds — **3+ years of self-directed, hands-on learning**, no formal CS degree, just projects that forced me to actually understand what I was building.

I go by **Rakuu**. I'm currently a **Frontend Developer Intern at AshvadTech**, based out of **Ranikhet, Uttarakhand** — small hill town, big debugging sessions. 🏔️

What makes me a little different: I don't just build to-do lists and portfolios to "learn React." I build things I'd actually want to use — a **Hinglish-speaking voice AI assistant**, a **family-tree platform that derives relationships through graph traversal**, a **secure notes marketplace with real payment integration**. I'd rather ship something ambitious and messy than something safe and shallow.

```yaml
philosophy: "Build it end-to-end, or don't build it"
career_goal: "Full Stack AI Engineer"
currently: ["Frontend polish", "Python", "AI Automation", "System design basics"]
```

- 🎨 I care about **detail** — glassmorphism, motion, micro-interactions, not just "does it work"
- 🧠 I like projects with **real architecture** — routing logic, DB schemas, multi-file structure — not single-file hacks
- 🗣️ I built an AI assistant that thinks in **Hindi/Hinglish**, not just English
- 📐 Vanilla JS by choice when I can — I like knowing what's actually happening under the hood
- 🎯 Endgame: **Full Stack AI Engineer** — frontend craft + backend logic + AI that does real work
